Amhacks is a 73 hour, 3,397 mile hackathon which takes place aboard the California Zephyr. The journey begins in New York City on Thursday June 19th and ends in San Francisco on Sunday June 22nd. You've never seen breathtaking views from behind your laptop screen like this before.
